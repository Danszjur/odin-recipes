Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian classic known for its comforting layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It is a hearty dish that brings together simple ingredients to create a flavorful and satisfying meal perfect for family dinners or special occasions. This recipe strikes a balance between a savory tomato-based meat sauce and smooth layers of pasta and cheese, baked until golden brown on top. The result is a warm, filling dish that pairs well with fresh salad or garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef or beef-pork mix
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 700 ml (24 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 250 g (9 oz) ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 200 g (7 oz) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50 g (2 oz) Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 °C (350 °F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground meat and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom.
  7. Add a layer of noodles, then spread ricotta, some meat sauce, and a sprinkle of mozzarella.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 15–20 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbling.
  10. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
